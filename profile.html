About me

I am a freelance translator, frelance interpreter, and translation researcher. I have offered translation and interpreting services to my clients for over ten years. My research project investigated how the English humour in a British sitcom was translated into subtitles in Traditional Chinese used in Taiwan. It might be surprising to some people that I had to quote a variety of jokes in order to finish my thesis. ;)
