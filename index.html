About me

I am a freelance translator and interpreter, and I also do translation research. I have offered translation and interpreting services to my clients for over ten years. My PhD research investigated how the English humour in a British sitcom was translated into Traditional Chinese subtitles. It might be surprising to some people that I had to quote a variety of jokes in order to finish my thesis. ;)
